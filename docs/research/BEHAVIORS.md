# Behaviors: Niche.com

## Interaction Model: Click-Driven
The site is primarily navigational. Most animations are state-based (hover/click) rather than scroll-triggered.

## Scroll Behaviors
- **Header**: Fixed at the top initially, scrolls out of view. *Correction from Recon*: Check if it becomes sticky on scroll-up or if it stays gone.
- **Fade-ins**: Sections appear to have subtle opacity transitions as they come into view.

## Interactive Components
- **Search Pills**: Switching between "K-12", "Colleges", and "Grad Schools" changes the search context.
- **Carousel**: "Real Niche Stories" section. Drag/Swipe enabled or button-triggered.
- **Hover States**:
  - Buttons: Slight scale or shift in background opacity.
  - Links: Text color change or underline.
  - Cards: Box shadow increase or subtle lift.

## Mobile Specifics
- **Menu**: Hamburger menu replaces top nav.
- **Stacking**: Grid layouts switch to single column.
